I am Raychel Morgan or Ray. I am an up an coming comic book artist with background in Graphic Design and Animation. I will be graudating LCC in August with a Assoicates of Graphic Communcations I have been on the midwest convention circuit since 2016 and plan to expand in the 2019. I love doing conventions and hope to one day do them full time. I love going to conventions that are non profit and for charity and will always choose these cons over larger ones. if you'd like to keep up with my schedule follow me on social media. I pride myself on being able to do a large variety of art and be well versed in many different styles. My work is influcenced by Marvel comics, Sanagrio, Alan Moore, Alphonse Mucha, and Greg Cappullo.

If you have questions about commissions or bulk orders on products feel free to contact me at my email or on any social media.
